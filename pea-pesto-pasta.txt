Ingredients:
 - 12 to 16 oz. pasta, cooked & drained
 - 1 bag frozen peas, thawed
 - fresh mint
 - grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
 - lemon juice
 - olive oil
 - salt & pepper

Directions:
  1. Thaw peas if haven't already done so.
  2. Blend peas with fresh mint and cheese, thinning with olive oil and lemon juice to reach desired consistency and taste.
  3. Add salt & pepper to taste.
  4. Toss with pasta and serve.
